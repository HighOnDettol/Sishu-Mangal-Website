About Dr. Prabhat Kumar Saha (Founder)

Dr. Prabhat Kumar Saha is the visionary founder of Sishu Mangal Parsat. His unwavering dedication and profound commitment to education laid the cornerstone for this esteemed organization. With a deep belief in nurturing young minds and fostering holistic development, Dr. Saha embarked on a mission to create accessible and quality educational opportunities for the community.

"......." - Dr. Prabhat Kumar Saha

Mission, Vision, and Motto

Our Mission

To provide quality education at low cost to the people of the underpriviledged community that empowers every child to achieve their full potential, fostering critical thinking, creativity, and a lifelong love for learning.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of educational excellence, creating a nurturing environment where students thrive academically, socially, and emotionally, becoming responsible global citizens.

Our Motto

"Health, Education, Nutrition"
